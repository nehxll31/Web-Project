FURNITURE

Scandinavian furniture

Scandinavian style furniture is minimalist and practical. Designers often use only solid wood to craft chair, tables, and other furniture items with a Nordic style. Natural materials like leather, linen, and Kvadrat textiles complement wood surfaces.

Modern furniture

Modern furniture typically features simple, basic shapes like squares, rectangles, and circles. Pieces are often designed to fit together like a puzzle, making them aesthetically pleasing as well as functional.

Rustic furniture

Rustic design is a style that's natural, aged, organic, and maybe even a little distressed. Design styles like farmhouse, Tuscan, or coastal can also be described as rustic. Rustic is the opposite of modern or contemporary design
