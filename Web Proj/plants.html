INDOOR PLANTS

Snake plant

The snake plant, commonly referred to as mother-in-law's tongue, is a resilient succulent that can grow anywhere between 6 inches to several feet.

Aloe vera

The botanical name of Aloe vera is Aloe barbadensis miller. It belongs to Asphodelaceae (Liliaceae) family, and is a shrubby or arborescent, perennial, xerophytic, succulent, pea- green color plant.

Peace lily

Peace lilies are sturdy, easy to grow plants with glossy, dark green oval leaves that narrow to a point. The leaves rise directly from the soil. These plants also periodically produce lightly fragrant white flowers that resemble calla lilies.
