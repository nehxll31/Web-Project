FALSE CEILINGS

Plaster of Paris False Ceiling

The Plaster of Paris false ceilings have a high durability and can be shaped and designed as per the requirements.

Wooden False Ceiling

Wood is often used in false ceiling designs due to its natural patterns and textures. Wooden false ceiling gives a warm and rustic look to your room interior. Wooden panels come in the form of hollow blocks, panels, or boards, and plywood.

Fibre False Ceiling

Fibre false ceiling is gaining its popularity due to low cost and easy installation process. Fibre ceilings are always in high demand in commercial applications like showrooms etc.
