FLOORING

Hardwood Flooring

Hardwood Flooring is made from a single piece of solid wood, which is about 18-20mm thick. This solid wood may be from oak, cherry, or walnut. Hardwood flooring is simply installed by nailing the solid wood to a wooden subfloor.

Carpet Flooring

Wool carpets have a higher ability to resist moisture and stain, unlike other types. Nylon carpets can be easily recycled. Carpets made from acrylic are resistant to mildew, insects, and crushing. Polyester carpets are moisture resistant but vulnerable to stain.

Marble Flooring

Marble flooring is known as the most luxurious among prominent flooring types; it is elegant, neat, durable, and -most importantly- very versatile. It comes in various color options; beige, black, green, pink, brown, red, blue, and gold.
